2. DATA COLLECTION AND ANONYMITY

The App is designed for anonymity and does not collect, store, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) about you.

Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require user accounts, registration, names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Does not collect precise location data, contacts, photos, or messages from your device.
  • Does not use third-party analytics or user tracking SDKs.

3. USE OF THE PERPLEXITY AI API

The core function of the App is to generate riddles using the Perplexity AI API. This interaction involves transmitting data outside the App to Perplexity's servers.

Data Transmitted: When you request a new riddle, the App sends a brief, non-personal text prompt to the Perplexity API (e.g., "Generate a fun, short riddle"). This request does not include any information that identifies you or your device.

API Key Security: The developer API key used to communicate with Perplexity is secured within the application's code and is never exposed to the end-user.

Data Handling by Perplexity: Your interaction with the Perplexity AI API is subject to Perplexity's privacy policies. We encourage you to review Perplexity's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

4. ADVERTISING AND GOOGLE ADMOB

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob to support ongoing development and operation.

What Google AdMob Collects: Google AdMob may collect limited information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Your general location (country, region) based on IP address.
  • Device identifiers and ad-related user identifiers.
  • Advertising preferences and browsing patterns.
  • Interactions with ads shown in the App.

No Personal Information: Google AdMob does not collect your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information through the App. Ad targeting is based on anonymized device data and general demographics, not personal identity.

Opting Out: You can limit ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad settings
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (toggle off)

Data Handling by Google: Google's use of ad data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it for complete details on ad-related data practices.

5. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION

All data storage related to your activity is minimal and temporary:

In-App Memory: The App temporarily stores the current riddle and answer in your device's memory for display purposes. It also maintains an in-memory history of recently seen riddles to minimize repetition within a single session.

Data Deletion: This temporary session data is immediately cleared when you fully close the App or explicitly reset the history. No long-term persistent storage (like cookies, local storage, or databases) is used to track session history or store riddles between launches.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The App is a riddle game intended for a general audience and is designed to generate content suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, they should contact us immediately at the email provided in Section 1.
