2. DATA COLLECTION AND ANONYMITY

The App is designed for anonymity and does not collect, store, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) about you.

Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require user accounts, registration, names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Does not collect precise location data, contacts, photos, or messages from your device.
  • Does not use third-party analytics or user tracking SDKs.

3. USE OF THE PERPLEXITY AI API

The core function of the App is to generate riddles using the Perplexity AI API. This interaction involves transmitting data outside the App to Perplexity's servers.

Data Transmitted: When you request a new riddle, the App sends a brief, non-personal text prompt to the Perplexity API (e.g., "Generate a fun, short riddle"). This request does not include any information that identifies you or your device.

API Key Security: The developer API key used to communicate with Perplexity is secured within the application's code and is never exposed to the end-user.

Data Handling by Perplexity: Your interaction with the Perplexity AI API is subject to Perplexity's privacy policies. We encourage you to review Perplexity's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

4. ADVERTISING AND GOOGLE ADMOB

The App displays non-intrusive advertisements through Google AdMob to support ongoing development and operation. These ads are served by Google's advertising network and may include banner ads and interstitial ads.

What Google AdMob Collects: Google AdMob may collect limited information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Your general location (country, region) based on IP address for geo-targeted advertising.
  • Device identifiers and ad-related user identifiers for frequency capping and ad measurement.
  • Advertising preferences and browsing patterns for ad personalization.
  • Interactions with ads shown in the App for performance analytics.

No Personal Information: Google AdMob does not collect your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information through the App. Ad targeting is based on anonymized device data and general demographics, not personal identity.

Data We Do NOT Receive: We do not have access to any of the data collected by Google AdMob. This information is used solely by Google for ad serving and measurement purposes.

Opting Out: You can limit ad personalization through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad settings
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (toggle off)

Additional Opt-out Options:

  • Ad Choices: Visit optout.aboutads.info to opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies.
  • Network Advertising Initiative: Visit optout.networkadvertising.org for additional opt-out options.

Data Handling by Google: Google's use of ad data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Google AdMob's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review these policies for complete details on ad-related data practices.

5. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION

All data storage related to your activity is minimal and temporary:

In-App Memory: The App temporarily stores the current riddle and answer in your device's memory for display purposes. It also maintains an in-memory history of recently seen riddles to minimize repetition within a single session.

Data Deletion: This temporary session data is immediately cleared when you fully close the App or explicitly reset the history. No long-term persistent storage (like cookies, local storage, or databases) is used to track session history or store riddles between launches.

6. SECURITY MEASURES

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we process:

API Communication: All communication with the Perplexity AI API is conducted over encrypted HTTPS connections to ensure data security in transit.

API Key Protection: The developer API key used to access the Perplexity AI service is securely embedded within the application code and is not accessible to end users.

Local Data Security: Any temporary data stored in your device's memory is cleared immediately upon app closure and is not written to persistent storage.

Third-Party Security: While we implement security measures for our part of the data processing, we rely on Google AdMob and Perplexity AI to maintain appropriate security measures for their respective data processing activities.

7. USER RIGHTS

Since we do not collect or store any personally identifiable information about you, your privacy rights are inherently protected. However, you have the following rights regarding your interaction with the App:

Right to Opt Out of Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings or the third-party opt-out mechanisms listed in Section 4.

Right to Data Deletion: All temporary session data is automatically deleted when you close the App. You can also manually clear the App's cache through your device settings if needed.

Right to Access Information: This Privacy Policy provides complete transparency about what data we do and do not collect, how we use it, and with whom we share it.

Right to Withdraw Consent: You can stop using the App at any time, which effectively withdraws any implied consent for data processing.
